dudhbhat mode

dudhbhat rules:

when active with 5+ players, one player is designated the dudhbhat (dummy). they get no secret word, but must listen to clues, pretend (larp) to have the word, and blend in. if exiled, they get a final chance to guess the civilian word for a side victory!

gupto_help.txt

how to play

1. memorize word: everyone gets a secret word. civilians get the same word, but guptos get a slightly different but related word.

2. describe clues: players go around giving a single word or short clue. guptos must blend in by guessing the civilian word, while civilians must detect the impostor without giving their word away.

3. discuss & vote: discuss who seems out of place, point fingers physically, and exile the suspect!

4. win & loss: civilians win if all guptos are exiled. guptos win if they match or outnumber civilians.

strategy tips

  • civilians: don't be too obvious! if the gupto deduces your word, they will copy your clues.
  • guptos: listen carefully. adapt descriptions to match what others are describing.
